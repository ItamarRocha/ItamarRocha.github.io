About me


I am a Brazilian Computer Engineering student at the Federal University of Paraíba - UFPB. My studying focus is on Artificial Intelligence and Operations Research and I love business and innovation.

I'm a dedicated and hard-working student, researcher, and intern (currently Data Science Intern at Armazém Paraíba), which is one of the biggest companies of the Brazilian northeast with 65 stores.

My main interests nowadays lay on Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, and Optimization.

Bass tab generator

Project of Operations Research Class

Medway is a middle-sized (300 students) course at João Pessoa - PB. The problem here consists in allocating each student to one of the 15 advisors based on the time needed by the student and the estimated compatibility between each pair to let things balanced and not overload any advisor.
